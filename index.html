Frequently Asked Questions

Is the app free to use? expand_more

Yes! Active Residents is 100% free for residents. We are funded by local councils to help them manage their workflow more efficiently.

Which councils are supported? expand_more

We support reporting to all UK local authorities. If your specific council hasn't fully integrated with our real-time status API yet, we send the report via email and track it manually.

Can I report anonymously? expand_more

Yes. While creating an account helps you track history, you can submit "Guest Reports" which do not require personal details.
